Gym Tracker
The rule
If the effort felt below 7/10 — increase weight next session. That's it.
Sets + reps
2 sets, 6–8 reps. Low volume, strength focused, progressive overload. Two quality sets done heavy beats five sets done light.
Goal
Not to be exhausted. To be challenged. You should feel like you did real work — not like you need to be carried out.
Finisher — every session
Treadmill: 5–10 min · 3.5 speed · 15% incline. Active recovery. Do not skip.
Changes happen outside the gym
Log during the session. Review weights and progress after — not at the machine. Adjust on Sundays.
Missed session
One missed session is not a failure. Just pick up where you left off — same day in the rotation. Don't restart, don't punish. Continue.
